Faith & Technology

Using digital platforms as a way to spread hope, kindness, and positive values — becoming a light in the online world.

Purpose of This Page

This webpage shows how technology can be used responsibly to promote faith, integrity, and compassion instead of negativity or misinformation.

“Let your light shine before others, that they may see your good deeds.” — Matthew 5:16

Faith in the Digital World

Social media, websites, and digital tools can be used to inspire others by sharing encouraging messages, respectful discussions, and truthful content.

Responsible Digital Citizenship

• Posting positive and respectful content
• Avoiding cyberbullying and harmful speech
• Giving credit to original creators
• Using technology to help and uplift others

Why This Matters

In a world where technology is part of daily life, our online actions should reflect our values. Being responsible online means becoming a source of light rather than darkness.
